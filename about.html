About PageRX

The conversion-first AI landing page analyzer. Built because most tools miss what actually matters: whether your page drives business results.

The Problem

Landing pages are the #1 conversion bottleneck for almost every startup and SaaS business. A 1% improvement in conversion rate can double revenue — yet most founders ship pages that leak 80-90% of their traffic without knowing why.

Existing tools fall into two camps. Speed checkers tell you if your page loads fast. SEO tools tell you if your meta tags are right. Neither tells you what actually matters: will this page convince a visitor to take action?

We kept seeing the same pattern. Founders spending weeks on perfect copy, running A/B tests, buying more traffic — all while their landing page had fundamental business viability issues: no clear value proposition, weak trust signals, confusing CTAs, missing social proof. Things no speed test or SEO checker would ever catch.

Most landing page tools check everything except what drives revenue. We built PageRX to fix that.

So we set out to build something different: an analysis that treats business viability as the primary lens — not an afterthought. One that looks at the same page through five distinct expert perspectives and scores it on what actually converts.

"Make world-class landing page analysis accessible to every founder."

No expensive CRO consultants. No bloated enterprise platforms. Just honest, data-driven analysis that tells you what's wrong and how to fix it — in 60 seconds, for free.

— The PageRX Team
