Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PageRX.

What exactly does PageRX analyze on my page?
PageRX analyzes your landing page from 5 expert perspectives — each one gives you a score out of 10 and prioritized action items:
🔒 Security — Checks for XSS vulnerabilities, CSP headers, HTTPS enforcement, mixed content warnings, and common security misconfigurations.
📐 Code Quality — Evaluates HTML semantics, JavaScript bloat, CSS efficiency, performance bottlenecks, and HTML validation errors.
🎨 UX — Assesses accessibility (WCAG compliance), mobile responsiveness, navigation clarity, visual hierarchy, and readability.
📋 Completeness — Scans for missing meta tags, Open Graph data, favicon, schema markup, sitemap references, and broken links.
💼 Business Viability — Reviews CTA placement and clarity, social proof presence, value proposition effectiveness, and conversion path optimization.
Do I need an account to analyze a page?
No! The free scan is available to anyone without an account, email, or credit card. Just enter any public URL and get your results in seconds. We believe in showing value before asking for anything in return.
How is the score calculated?
Each of the 5 perspectives is scored out of 10 based on the number and severity of issues found. The overall score is a weighted average scaled to 0-100. A score above 70 means your page is in good shape; between 50-70 means meaningful improvements are needed; below 50 indicates significant issues requiring attention. The heuristic scan gives you a quick baseline, while the Pro LLM analysis provides deeper, more nuanced scoring.
What's the difference between the free scan and Pro?
The free scan uses heuristic analysis (rule-based detection) — it's fast (<3s) and catches well-known issues with high accuracy. It gives you a solid baseline understanding of your page's health.
Pro uses LLM-powered analysis — each of the 5 reviews runs through a different specialized AI model, giving you deeper, more nuanced insights that catch subtle issues heuristic rules miss. Pro also includes unlimited scans, PDF report exports, and weekly monitoring to track your page's health over time.
Can I use PageRX on multiple pages?
Absolutely! The free tier gives you 10 scans per month — enough to test multiple pages or run a few audits. With a Pro subscription, you get unlimited scans — analyze as many pages as you need, as often as you want.
Is my data safe? What do you store?
Yes. We only analyze the HTML content you submit in real-time. We don't store your page content permanently. Our analysis runs in memory and is not shared with third parties. We do collect anonymous analytics (page views, feature usage) to improve the product — no personal data, no cookies. For the lead capture modal, we store only your email and the URL you analyzed to send you the PDF report.
How accurate is the AI analysis?
The heuristic analysis catches well-known issues with high accuracy — it's deterministic and consistent. The LLM-powered Pro analysis uses state-of-the-art models (Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini) for deeper understanding and nuanced feedback. No automated analysis is perfect, so we always recommend reviewing findings critically — but early users report that PageRX catches 85-90% of the issues a human expert would find, in a fraction of the time.
I'm on the free plan. Can I upgrade later?
Yes, anytime. Your scans and data are preserved when you upgrade. Just click "Subscribe" on the Pro or Agency plan, complete your payment via Stripe, and your account is instantly upgraded. No disruption, no data loss.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes — we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If PageRX isn't delivering value for your workflow, contact us and we'll issue a full refund. No questions asked.
