Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PageRX.

What does PageRX analyze?
PageRX analyzes your landing page from 5 expert perspectives: Security (XSS, CSP, vulnerabilities), Code Quality (patterns, performance, maintainability), UX (accessibility, mobile-friendliness), Completeness (missing elements, SEO tags), and Business Viability (CTA, social proof, conversion optimization).
Do I need an account to analyze a page?
No! The free scan is available to anyone without an account. Just enter a URL and get your results in seconds.
How is the score calculated?
Each of the 5 perspectives is scored out of 10 based on issues found and severity. The overall score is a weighted average scaled to 0-100. A score above 70 means your page is in good shape; below 50 means significant improvements are needed.
What's the difference between free and Pro?
The free scan uses heuristic analysis (rule-based detection). Pro uses LLM-powered analysis — each of the 5 reviews runs through a different AI model specialized for that perspective, giving deeper, more nuanced insights. Pro also includes PDF reports and weekly monitoring.
Can I use PageRX on multiple pages?
Absolutely! With a Pro subscription, you have unlimited scans. The free tier gives you 10 scans per month.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We only analyze the HTML content you submit. We don't store your page content permanently. Our analysis runs in real-time and is not shared with third parties.
How accurate is the AI analysis?
The heuristic analysis catches well-known issues with high accuracy. The LLM-powered analysis uses state-of-the-art models (including Claude, GPT-4o, and Gemini) for deeper understanding. No automated analysis is perfect, so we always recommend reviewing the findings and suggestions critically.
